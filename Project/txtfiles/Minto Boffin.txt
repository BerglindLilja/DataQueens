'''Minto Boffin''' was a member of the [[Boffin]] family.

He is a character invented for [[Peter Jackson]]’s film ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring]]''.

== Portrayal in adaptations ==

=== Peter Jackson’s ''The Fellowship of the Ring'' ===
In [[TA 3001]], Minto and his sister [[Bell Boffin|Bell]] attended [[Bilbo Baggins]]'s farewell party. While at the party, he and a group of other young [[hobbit]] children listened to Bilbo's tale about he and a [[Thorin and Company|company of]] [[Dwarves]] were captured by [[Stone-trolls]].
