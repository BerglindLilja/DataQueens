'''Linnar, Sindri, and Uri''' were three non-canonical [[Dwarves]] of the [[Second Age]] who each received one of the [[Rings of Power|Dwarven Rings of Power]].

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
=== The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game ===

They were created in the ''[[The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game]]'', and were each royal lords after they received their Rings. The mined for [[Mithril]] and [[Gold]] and [[Silver]]. Their Rings eventually faded, but it is not known whether they were eaten by dragons or somehow fell into the hands of [[Sauron]].
