'''Silindë''' was an [[Elf]] of [[Rivendell]], serving as an assistant to Lord [[Elrond]].

== Biography ==
During the [[War of the Ring]], Elrond invited Silindë to attend the Council of [[Elrond]], which would decide the fate of the [[Ring of Power]]. He helped Elrond conclude that the One Ring must be destroyed in the fires of [[Mount Doom]]. After [[Sauron]]'s death, resulting the destruction of the One Ring which was thrown into the fires of Mount Doom by the [[Hobbits|Hobbit]] [[Frodo Baggins]], Silindë attended the coronation at [[Minas Tirith]], and felt very happy for [[Aragorn]] and [[Arwen]]. Later on, Silindë eventually went to the [[Undying Lands]] with some of his people while the rest chose to stay throughout the [[Fourth Age]].
