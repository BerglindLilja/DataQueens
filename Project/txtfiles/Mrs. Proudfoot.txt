'''Mrs. Proudfoot''' was a [[Hobbit]] of the [[Shire]].

== Biography ==
She married [[Odo Proudfoot]] at some point prior to [[TA 2946]], when their son [[Olo Proudfoot|Olo]] was born. Little to nothing else is known of her life, including when she died.<ref>[[The Lord of the Rings|''The Lord of the Rings'']]: [[Appendix C]], "Baggins of Hobbiton"</ref>

== Portrayal in adaptations ==

In Peter Jackson's film [[The Fellowship of the Ring (film)|''The Fellowship of the Ring'']]'', ''Mrs. Proudfoot has a non-speaking role, appearing only during the "Shire Introduction" to non-verbally chastise her husband for enjoying the firework-antics of the wizard [[Gandalf]], perceived disturber of the peace.<ref>''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring (film)]]''</ref>
