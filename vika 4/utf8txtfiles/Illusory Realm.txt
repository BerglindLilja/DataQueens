The {{Term/Store|Illusory Realm|Illusory Realms|Series, BotW}} is a location in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|Illusory Realm<br>{{Big|Fireblight Ganon}}|N/A|BotW}}</ref> It is exclusive to {{TCB}} DLC Pack.

==Features and Overview==
{{Stub|BotW}}
The {{Term|BotW|Illusory Realm}} is a world created from {{Term|BotW|Link|link}}'s memories and fears.<ref>{{Cite|This illusory realm was created from the depths of your memories... The enemy you shall face is a product of the fear that dwells within. You must overcome this fear to proceed. This battle is a trial of the soul. You must emerge victorious using only the tools provided.|Monk Maz Koshia|BotW}}</ref> It is visited four times during four {{Plural|BotW|Main Quest|link|display=Quests}}: during the "{{Term|BotW|EX Champion Daruk's Song|link}}", "{{Term|BotW|EX Champion Mipha's Song|link}}", "{{Term|BotW|EX Champion Urbosa's Song|link}}", and "{{Term|BotW|EX Champion Revali's Song|link}}". During each of these {{Plural|BotW|Main Quest|display=Quests}}, {{Term|BotW|Link}} must face {{Term|BotW|Fireblight Ganon|link}} in a replica of {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Rudania|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Waterblight Ganon|link}} in a replica of {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Medoh|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Thunderblight Ganon|link}} in a replica of {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Naboris|link}}, and {{Term|BotW|Windblight Ganon|link}}  in a replica of {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Ruta|link}} with a pre-determined set of {{Plural|BotW|Weapon|link}} and {{Plural|BotW|Armor|link}}. The {{Term|BotW|Illusory Realm}} is marked by a hazy cloud which partially inhibits {{Term|BotW|Link}}'s vision.

The equipment that {{Term|BotW|Link}} has in each visit to the {{Term|BotW|Illusory Realm}} is as follows:
