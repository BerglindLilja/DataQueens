'''Rúmil''' was an [[Galadhrim|Elf of Lórien]] and brother of [[Orophin]] and [[Haldir of Lothlórien|Haldir]], the leading [[Marchwarden]] of Lórien, during the [[Third Age]].

== Biography ==
[[File:FOTR - Lorien Brothers.jpg|thumb|left|260px|Rúmil and his brothers]]

Rúmil was a marchwarden of [[Lothlórien|Lórien]], and was one who guarded the border of the western part of the forest, with his brothers. He was present at the [[Fellowship of the Ring (group)|Fellowship of the Ring]]'s first meeting with the [[Galadhrim]].

Later, he and Haldir guided the Fellowship to [[Caras Galadhon]]. Rúmil spoke very little [[Westron]] and so could not significantly interact with the Fellowship when he and his brothers encountered them in the [[forest]].<ref>''[[The Lord of the Rings]], [[The Fellowship of the Ring]]'', Book Two, Chapter VI: "[[Lothlorien]]"</ref>

== Etymology ==

According to [[Robert Foster]], the name '''''Rúmil''''' is [[Quenya]].<ref>''[[The Complete Guide to Middle-earth]]''</ref>

== Early versions of the legendarium ==

In early drafts of ''[[The Lord of the Rings]]'', Rúmil had the names ''Rhimbron'', ''Romrin'', and ''Rhomrin''.<ref>''[[The History of Middle-earth]]'', Vol. VII: ''[[The Treason of Isengard]]'', XII: "Lothlórien"</ref> As Rhimbron, he had another companion named Hathaldir.<ref>''[[The History of Middle-earth]]'', Vol. VII: ''[[The Treason of Isengard]]'', XII: "Lothlórien", Notes</ref>

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
=== The Lord of the Rings film trilogy ===
In [[Peter Jackson]]'s ''[[The Fellowship of the Ring (film)|The Fellowship of the Ring]]'' [[The Fellowship of the Ring (film)|film]], Rúmil is portrayed by the Danish-born [[New Zealand]] actor [[Jørn E. Benzon]], the older brother of [[Glorfindel]]'s actor, [[Jarl Benzon]].

=== The Lord of the Rings: TCG ===

In ''[[The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game]]'', Rúmil is identified as the brother of Haldir, a Silvan Elf, and an Elven Protector.

=== Video games ===
*In ''[[The Lord of the Rings: War of the Ring]]'' by Games Workshop, he can be fielded as part of the Guardians of [[Caras Galadhon]] Legendary Formation.

*In ''[[The Lord of the Rings Online]]'', Rúmil can be found on the western outskirts of [[Lothlorien|Lothl]][[Galadhrim|órien]], defending the borders against the [[Orc]] attacks.
