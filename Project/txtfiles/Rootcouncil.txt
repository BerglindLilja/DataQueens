'''Rootcouncil''' was a wise beech-like [[Ent]] who lived in [[Fangorn Forest]]. He was among those that attended the [[Entmoot]], which was consulted by [[Treebeard]], and discussed on whethere the Ents should go to war against [[Saruman]]. Later, Rootcouncil fought in the [[Battle of Isengard|battle of]] [[Isengard]] where he and hundreds of Ents battled Saruman's [[orc]]s.

His name originates from Peter Jackson's [[The Lord of the Rings film trilogy|Lord of the Rings film trilogy]], and so he is a non-canon character.
