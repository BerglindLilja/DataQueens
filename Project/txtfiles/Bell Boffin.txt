'''Bell Boffin''' was a member of the [[Boffin]] family.

== Biography ==
In the year [[TA 3001]], Bell and her brother [[Minto Boffin|Minto]] attended [[Bilbo Baggins]]' [[Bilbo's Farewell Birthday Party|Farewell Birthday Party]]. At the party, she and a group of other young [[hobbit]] children listened to Bilbo's tale about how he and a [[Thorin and Company|company of]] [[Dwarves]], led by [[Thorin II Oakenshield]], were captured by the [[Stone-trolls]].

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
Bell Boffin is portrayed by [[Katie Jackson]], the daughter of director [[Peter Jackson]], who created the [[Lord of the Rings film trilogy]].

[[Katie Jackson]] later portrayed [[Betsy Butterbur]] in [[The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug|''The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug'']].
