{{Term/Store|Mount Taran||Series, BotW}} is a location in {{BotW}}.<ref>{{Cite|'''Mount Taran'''|Map|BotW}}</ref>

==Features and Overview==
Mount Taran is located in [[East Necluda]]. It is home to the [[Ancient Shrine|Tawa Jinn Shrine]] and the three [[Giant Brothers]]. There are three pedestals for orbs and a stone tablet that instructs Link on how to complete the shrine quest [[The Three Giant Brothers]]<ref>{{Cite|The {{Color|BotW Red|ancient orbs}} guarded by the giants of Mount Taran lead to the {{Color|BotW Red|shine}}|Stone Tablet|BotW}}</ref>. On and around the mountain are a few patches of marshland surrounded by what appear to be giant ribs of some long-dead creature. Three of the marshlands ([[Hanu Pond]], [[Uten Marsh]] and [[Rabella Wetlands]]) contains a hinox, each of whom have a [[Ancient Orb]] on their necklace.
