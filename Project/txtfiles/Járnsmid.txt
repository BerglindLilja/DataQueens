'''Járnsmid'''<ref>http://lotrtcgwiki.com/wiki/lotr02025</ref> was a merchant from the [[city]] of [[Dale]].

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
=== Peter Jackson's The Lord of the Rings film trilogy ===

During the [[War of the Ring]], Járnsmid was sent as an emissary to attend the [[Council of Elrond]], discussing on what it is to be done with the [[One Ring]].
