'''Erland''' was an elderly adviser who served under King [[Brand]], son of [[Bain]], at the time of the [[War of the Ring]]. 

His character was created for [[The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game|''The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game'']], and as such is non-canonical. 

==Portrayal in adaptations==
===Peter Jackson's The Fellowship of the Ring===

Erland was one of those that attended the [[Council of Elrond]], discussing what was to be done with the [[One Ring]].
