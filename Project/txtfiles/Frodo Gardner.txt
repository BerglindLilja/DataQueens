'''Frodo Gardner''', also known as '''Frodo Gamgee''', was the son of [[Samwise Gamgee]] and [[Rosie Cotton|Rosie Gamgee]]. He was named after [[Frodo Baggins]], and took the last name of Gardner after his father became the [[Shire]]'s gardener. He owned [[Bag End]] for a time.

== Biography ==

The son of Sam Gamgee, Frodo was born in the year [[FO 2]] and was the second child and eldest son of Sam and Rosie Cotton Gamgee. He had an older sister, [[Elanor Gardner|Elanor]], and eleven younger siblings: [[Rose Gardner|Rose]], [[Merry Gardner|Merry]], [[Pippin Gardner|Pippin]], [[Goldilocks (Gardner) Took|Goldilocks]], [[Hamfast Gardner|Hamfast]], [[Daisy Gardner|Daisy]], [[Primrose Gardner|Primrose]], [[Bilbo Gardner|Bilbo]], [[Ruby Gardner|Ruby]], [[Robin Gardner|Robin]], and [[Tolman Gardner|Tolman]].

Sam Gamgee came to be known as Sam Gardner because of his skill at gardening and his role in replanting the Shire after the [[War of the Ring]]. His son Frodo took Gardner as his family name.
[[File:Frodogamgee.jpg|250px|thumb|left|Frodo Gardner as a child with his mother Rosie in the movies]]
Frodo Gardner probably inherited Bag End after his father left [[Middle-earth]] in the year [[FO 61]]. From Frodo were descended the [[Gardner|Gardners]] of the Hill including his son Holfast Gardner, born in [[FO 41]], and his grandson [[Harding of the Hill]].<ref>''[[The Lord of the Rings]],'' [[Appendix C]]: Family Trees, The Longfather - Tree of Master Samwise</ref>

== Portrayal in adaptations ==

Frodo Gardner appeared in the arms of Rosie (played by [[Sarah McLeod]]). He is played by [[Maisy McLeod-Riera]], who is the real-life daughter of Sarah McLeod.

=== Video games ===

Frodo Gamgee is a playable character in the Wii and PlayStation 3 version of ''[[Lord Of The Rings: Aragorn's Quest]]'', particularly in the Shire overworld apart from Aragorn's eight story levels.
