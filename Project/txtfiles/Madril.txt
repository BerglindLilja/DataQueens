'''Madril''' was a [[Rangers of Ithilien|Ranger of Ithilien]] under the command [[Faramir]], at [[Henneth Annûn]] and at [[Osgiliath]].

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
=== Peter Jackson's The Lord of the Rings trilogy ===

Madril was both an adviser and a second-in-command to Faramir. He was with Faramir when [[Sam]] and [[Frodo]] were captured by his men. When Faramir decided to let them go, Madril reminded him that if he failed to deliver the [[One Ring|Ring]] to [[Gondor]] he risked losing his life.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0000165/?ref_=nm_flmg_act_21</ref> He accompanies Faramir on his mission of the [[Battle of Osgiliath]]. He commanded a group of archers during the Battle of Osgiliath but he suffered a head wound. As Faramir called for retreat, Madril was cut down by an [[Orc]] of [[Minas Morgul]], being severely wounded during the battle, and was thought by Faramir to be dead when the [[Men of Gondor]] retreat at the Ringwraiths' arrival. He is then seen lying defenseless on the ground, before being killed by [[Gothmog (Lieutenant of Morgul)|Gothmog]] with an Orcish spear.

== Video games ==
=== The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King ===

In ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King (video game)|The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King]]'' video game, Madril appears through the use of archival footage.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0387360/</ref>  

=== LEGO the Lord of the Rings ===

In ''[[LEGO The Lord of the Rings: The Video Game|LEGO The Lord of the Rings]]'', Madril appears in Level 13, The [[Dead Marshes]], as a playable character along with [[Faramir]].
<ref>http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2283948/fullcredits?ref_=tt_cl_sm#cast</ref>

== Table-Top ==

=== Lord of the Rings: Strategy Battle Game ===
In ''The Lord of the Rings: Strategy Battle Game'' book "Shadow in the East", A younger Madril is introduced alongside Lieutenant Cirion during the closing years of the Third Age. Madril was on the scene for Gondor's very first encounters with Khand and [[Easterling]] forces that entered from Rhun and beyond.

== Behind the scenes ==

Madril is played in Peter Jackson's films by [[John Bach]].
