'''Unferth''' was one of [[Grima Wormtongue|Grima Wormtongue']]s bodyguards at [[Edoras]], and also a loyal agent to [[Saruman]] in the [[War of the Ring]].

He is not mentioned in the books, and is named solely for ''[[The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game]]''.<ref name="lotrtcgwiki">http://lotrtcgwiki.com/wiki/lotr04178</ref>
