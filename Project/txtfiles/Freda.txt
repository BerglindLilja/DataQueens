'''Freda''' was the daughter of [[Morwen (movie character)|Morwen]] and sister of [[Éothain (young Rohirrim)|Eothain]] in [[Rohan]].

The character is portrayed by [[Olivia Tennet]], a New Zealand actress, in [[Peter Jackson]]'s ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers]]''.

== Biography ==

During the [[War of the Ring]], Freda and her older brother Éothain were sent by their mother to alert King [[Théoden]] that the [[Dunlendings|Wild-men]] were raiding through [[Rohan]]. Upon arriving to [[Edoras]], Freda and Éothain were kept safe until Théoden led his people to [[Helm's Deep]]. They were reunited with their mother.
