'''Iorlas''' (b. [[TA 2990]]) was a man who lived in [[Minas Tirith]]. He was the uncle of [[Bergil]].

== Biography ==

While talking with [[Peregrin Took|Pippin]] who mentioned his age, Bergil said that Pippin was as old as his uncle Iorlas. 

==Portrayal in adaptations==
[[File:The Lord of the Rings Online - Iorlas.jpg|thumb|337x337px|Iorlas in ''The Lord of the Rings Online'']]
In ''[[The Lord of the Rings Online]]'' Iorlas is [[Beregond]]'s brother and is first found  on the seventh level of pre-battle [[Minas Tirith]]. During the siege of the city he is struck by a fallen stone, but is protected by his helmet, suffering only what he calls a "dizziness".
