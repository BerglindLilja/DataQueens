'''Diamond Took''' or '''Diamond of Long Cleeve''' was a [[Hobbit]] of the [[Shire]].

== Biography ==

Diamond was born in [[Long Cleeve]] in the [[Northfarthing]] to unnamed parents who were possibly one of the [[Took (family)|North-Tooks]], descended from [[Bandobras Took|Bandobras "Bullroarer" Took]]. She became the wife of [[Thain]] [[Peregrin Took]] in [[FO 6]] (SR 1427) about eight years after the [[War of the Ring]]. She had one son [[Faramir Took I|Faramir]], named for a [[Stewards of Gondor|Steward of Gondor]]. Her date of death is not known, but some people assume Diamond probably died sometime before the year [[FO 63]] when Pippin left the Shire to live in [[Gondor]].<ref>''The Lord of the Rings,'' [[Appendix C]], "Took of Great Smials"</ref><ref>''[[The Lord of the Rings]]'', [[Appendix B]], "Later events concerning the members of the Fellowship of the Ring"</ref>

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
=== The Lord of the Rings trilogy ===

Diamond was portrayed by [[Emma Deakin]] in the [[The Return of the King (film)|film adaptation]] of ''[[The Return of the King (novel)|The Return of the King]]''.
