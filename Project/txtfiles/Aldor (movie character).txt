'''Aldor''' was an elderly [[Rohirrim|Man of Rohan]].<ref>[http://lotrtcgwiki.com/wiki/lotr04262 ''http://lotrtcgwiki.com/wiki/lotr04262'']</ref>

== Portrayal in adaptations ==
=== ''The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers'' ===

Having fled with his people to the fortress of the [[Hornburg]] from [[Edoras]], he was among those forced to defend the fortress from [[Saruman]]'s [[Saruman's army|army]] of [[Uruk-hai]]. He stood beside [[Haleth (Son of Háma)|Haleth]], son of [[Háma]], where they witnessed the arrival of the Uruk-hai. Before the Uruk-hai began their attack, Aldor prepared to loose an arrow from his bow. However, he was unable to steady his hand and accidentally released the arrow, killing an Uruk, which preempted the attacking army's assault.

His fate in the subsequent [[Battle of the Hornburg]] is unknown.
