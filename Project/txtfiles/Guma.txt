'''Guma''' was a young farmer who lived in [[Rohan]] during the [[War of the Ring]].

He is non-canonical, as he only appears in ''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers]]''. His name was revealed in ''[[The Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game]]''.
==Biography==
After [[King of Rohan|King]] [[Theoden]] was released from [[Saruman]]'s control with the help of [[Gandalf|Gandalf the White]], Guma left [[Edoras]] with all the civilians and traveled to [[Helm's Deep]]. When [[Saruman]] sent an army of 10,000 [[Uruk-hai]] to destroy the people of Rohan, Guma, along with a number of farmers, had to fight and defend the fortress at all cost.

==Etymology==
''Guma'' is Old English for "the young man".
